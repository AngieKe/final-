Objectives¶

Coldplay's music has resonated with audiences worldwide, offering emotional support through its lyrics. This project aims to explore the theme of hope in Coldplay's song lyrics by identifying and analyzing keywords related to hope. Using text analysis techniques, I will visualize the most frequently used hope-related keywords through a bar chart to illustrate how the band conveys messages of optimism and encouragement in their music.
